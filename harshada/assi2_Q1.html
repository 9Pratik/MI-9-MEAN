From the garbage to the Googleplex

The Google story begins in 1995 at Stanford university.
Larry Page was considering Stanford for grad school and Sergey Brin, the student their, was assigned to show him around.

By some accounts, they disagreed about nearly everything during that first meeting,but by following year struct a partnership.
Working from their dorm rooms, they built a search engine that used links to determine the importance of individual page on the World Wide Web.

They called this search engine Backrub

Initial public offering

Google's initial public offering(IPO) took place five years later, on 19th August 2004.
At that time Larry Page, Sergey Brin, and Eric Schmidt agreed to work together at Google for 30 20years,until the year 2024.
